🎓 TEACHER WORKFLOW:

1. Open EduTrack → Click "📊 Assessment Matrix"
2. Select Grade 1, All Streams, Term 1, Opener
3. Matrix appears with all students & subjects
4. Click cell → Type mark (0-100) → Press Enter
5. Repeat for all cells (~30-40 min for 40 students)
6. All data saved locally
7. After 30s, syncs to Google Sheets automatically
8. Teacher can verify in Google Sheets


⚙️ ADMIN WORKFLOW:

1. Monitor assessment entry in matrix
2. Click "☁️ Sheet" button
3. Matrix created in Google Sheets (2-3 seconds)
4. Verify formatting and data
5. Export to CSV for backup
6. End of term: Create matrices for next term
